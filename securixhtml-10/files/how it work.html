How Our Digital Door Lock System Works in Vault Care

Step 1: Fingerprint Registration

Users need to register their fingerprint through the secure biometric scanner for future access.

Step 2: Face Recognition Setup

Facial data is scanned and stored securely, allowing seamless access without needing a key.

Step 3: Password & PIN Protection

Set up a strong PIN or password as an alternative way to unlock the vault.

Step 4: Remote Unlock Feature

Unlock your vault remotely through our mobile app for added convenience.

Step 5: Real-time Access Logs

Monitor access logs in real-time to ensure security and detect any unauthorized attempts.

Step 6: Emergency Override

In case of an emergency, an authorized person can use a master key or OTP verification for access.
